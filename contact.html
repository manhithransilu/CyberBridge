Connect With Us

Report a Security Concern

If you've encountered a phishing attempt, suspicious email, or potential security threat targeting students, we want to hear about it. Your reports help us warn the broader student community.

Report to: security@cyberbridge.edu

Please include as much detail as possible: screenshots of the suspicious content (don't click any links!), sender information, and the date/time you received it.

Frequently Asked Questions

My university account was hacked. What should I do?

Contact your university IT Service Desk immediately (134 ECU). They can lock your account, reset your password, and investigate the breach. After regaining access, enable MFA and review your account for any unauthorised changes.

Is there a cost for CyberBridge resources?

All CyberBridge resources are completely free for university students, staff, and faculty. Our mission is to make cybersecurity education accessible to everyone.

Can I use CyberBridge materials for my own presentations?

Yes! We encourage the sharing of cybersecurity knowledge. Please credit CyberBridge as the source and link back to our website where possible. For commercial use, please contact us first.
