About CyberBridge

CyberBridge is an educational initiative designed specifically for university students. We translate complex cybersecurity terminology into clear, relatable explanations grounded in real student experiences.

In today's interconnected world, students face unique digital challenges. From sharing devices in computer labs to connecting to campus Wi-Fi networks, your digital footprint is constantly at risk. CyberBridge helps you understand these risks and provides practical strategies to protect yourself.

This platform focuses on real-world scenarios including:

  • Safe campus Wi-Fi usage and public network security
  • Protecting shared devices in libraries and labs
  • Securing your learning management system accounts
  • Smart social media practices for students
  • Recognising phishing attempts targeting student accounts
